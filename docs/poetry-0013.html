Captain's Log: Ninth Day of Quarantine: I found one more chicken, am ready for dinner. I created a small Soundboard for what is basically a tiny collection of LOFI Hip-Hop sounds. Realistically it is difficult to call it music at this point, I have all the components but there were bugs along the way. Svelte Sapper is complicated to visualize, it pre-creates some things, meaning before I publish to the website, Sapper will create a pre-ran version of the site. When a visitor visits, the pre-ran (pre-generated static version) will then be upgraded to a real version, and replaced by now running live version. It is the correct technology, and it didn't get me, but it does take a moment to figure out when a bug jumps out of nowhere.
