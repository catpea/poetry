Nonfiction books are way to share wisdom,<br>
some wisdom is in the form of advice, other you have to discover yourself.

Life is too precious, and we are far too intelligent not to inherit wisdom from books,<br>
there is only one direction for us all, and that is towards Wisdom.

Growing up,<br>
enlarges our worlds.

Wisdom, helps us see more roads in our Journeys,<br>
roads that we would not have noticed without it.

<br><br>

How can we be sure if we are traveling on the path that does us justice,<br>
if we can't see all the forks and roads ahead?

<br><br>

Let pursuit of wisdom bring you the light needed to enlighten your path,<br>
to show you all the roads and many of the futures ahead.

Seek your own wisdom from many books that interest you,<br>
that present a coherent path of self education.

Connect them in harmony,<br>
make your road beautiful.

<br><br>

The reasons to break away from waiting to be taught and following others,<br>
are countless, and the access to books and audio-books, is too easy.

Nonfiction Best Seller Audio-books are especially powerful at teaching,<br>
some represent a near lifetime of knowledge, of rising to success and wisdom.

We don't just inherit knowledge that leads to wisdom,<br>
we also inherit culture, ways of thinking, and roads towards joy and happiness.

<br><br>

Humanity celebrates those who rise,<br>
it celebrates wisdom and the struggle for goodness.

The most beautiful of books and audio-books,<br>
very often end up on Best Seller lists, start there.

Find a way to listen to Nonfiction Best Sellers,<br>
and inherit the treasures within.
